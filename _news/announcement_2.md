Our paper [Vision-align-to-Language integrated Knowledge Graph (VaLiK)](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/ICCV2025/html/Liu_Aligning_Vision_to_Language_Annotation-Free_Multimodal_Knowledge_Graph_Construction_for_ICCV_2025_paper.html) has been accepted by **ICCV 2025**! VaLiK grounds vision in text and filters noise for annotation‑free MMKGs, boosting LLM reasoning to SOTA with high efficiency. Code available at [Link](https://github.com/Wings-Of-Disaster/VaLiK). 🤗🤗
